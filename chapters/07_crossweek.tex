\chapter{Cross-Week Concept Threads}
\label{ch:crossweek}

The course runs as two parallel threads. This chapter follows both end to end; read it when a question spans weeks.

\section{The Conceptual Track}
\label{sec:thread-conceptual}

Week~1 fixes the vocabulary — hypothesis, variable, study types (\autoref{sec:language-research}) — and the hourglass logic of deduction to measurement and induction back to generalisation (\autoref{sec:hourglass}). Week~2 turns that vocabulary into a design: the objective, research questions, and framework (\autoref{sec:objectives-rqs}), culminating in operationalisation (\autoref{sec:operationalization}), where abstract concepts become exact variables handed to the technical design (\autoref{sec:strategy-materials}). Week~6 closes the thread: the plan becomes the RM report body (\autoref{sec:reports}) and the Week~1 literature (\autoref{sec:research-ideas}) returns as a reference list meeting the sourcing and style rules of Week~6 (\autoref{sec:referencing}).

\section{The Empirical Track}
\label{sec:thread-empirical}

Week~1 names the sample and measure steps (\autoref{sec:research-process}). Week~3 makes them exact: the sample becomes the population-to-sample funnel with representativeness statistics (\autoref{sec:sampling}, \autoref{sec:sample-types}), and measurement becomes a treatment of data sources, legal categories, data types, quality checks, and the logbook (\autoref{sec:data-sources}). Week~4 interrogates the result through verification and validation (\autoref{sec:vv}) and the EOP framework (\autoref{sec:eop}), which reaches back to Week~3's standard error to demand that sample size be justified. Week~5 manages both the data and the project as the logbook grows into the DMP (\autoref{sec:dmp}) and the Week~2 plan becomes a WBS and Gantt chart (\autoref{sec:project-mgmt}).

\section{Where the Tracks Meet}
\label{sec:thread-meet}

The tracks converge at data management, which collects output from the empirical data work, the EOP resource judgement, and the technical design at once (\autoref{sec:dmp}). The RM report is the final meeting point: its body carries the conceptual plan while its conduct, sourcing, and data handling carry the empirical discipline (\autoref{ch:reportguide}).
